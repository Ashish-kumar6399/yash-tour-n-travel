Destination covered: Delhi–Agra–Jaipur– Pushkar–Udaipur –Jodhpur via Ranakpur – Jaisalmer – Bikaner – Mandawa – Delhi

Rajasthan Tour

Day :01 Delhi- Agra
  • After breakfast you will be transferred from Delhi for Agra approx 220 kms / 4hrs. Check in at your hotel upon arrival in Agra. Evening will be at own leisure. Overnight stay at hotel
    • Note: We have free Guide services in Jaipur.
Day : 02 Agra – Fatehpur Sikri – Jaipur
  • This morning visit Taj Mahal at sun rise after breakfast you will be transferred from Agra for Jaipur by surface approx 250kms / 5 hrs. Enroute visit Fatehpur Sikri and check in at your hotel upon arrival in Jaipur. Evening will be at own leisure. Overnight stay at hotel
Day :03 In Jaipur
  • After breakfast depart for Full day sightseeing of Jaipur and visit Amber Fort, City Palace, Jantar Mantar, Hawa Mahal, Museum and laxmi Narayan Temple. Evening will be free for shopping. Overnight stay at hotel
    • Note: We have free Guide services in Jaipur.
Day :04 Jaipur – Pushkar
  • This morning you will be transferred from Jaipur for Pushkar by surface approx 150kms / 3 hrs. Check in at your hotel upon arrival in Pushkar. Depart for half-day sightseeing and visit Holy Lake and Bhrama Temple. Evening will be at own leisure. Overnight stay at hotel
Day :05 Pushkar – Udaipur via Chittorgarh Fort
  • This morning you will be transferred from Pushkar for Udaipur by surface approx 300kms / 6 hrs. Check in at your hotel upon arrival in Udaipur. Enjoy the walking tours of narrow streets in the city and also visit washing ghats. Evening will be at own leisure. Overnight stay at hotel
Day :06 In Udaipur
  • Morning after breakfast depart for Full-day sightseeing of Udaipur and visit City Palace Museum enjoy boat ride (on own), Saheliyon ki Badi, Jagdish Temple and Fateh Sagar Lake. Evening will be at own leisure. Overnight stay at hotel
Day :07 Udaipur – Ranakpur – Jodhpur
  • This morning after breakfast depart for Jodhpur approx 300 kms / 6 hrs, Enroute visit Ranakpur temple. Check in at your hotel upon arrival in Jodhpur. Overnight stay at hotel
Day :08 In Jodhpur
  • This morning enjoy the full-day excursion of Jodhpur and visit Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada & Umaid Bhawan Palace. Evening will be at own leisure. Overnight stay at hotel
Day :09 Jodhpur – Jaisalmer
  • This morning after breakfast Proceed for Khuri Desert, Enjoy your welcome drink upon arrival. Enjoy the camel ride in the sand dunes. Overnight stay at Desert by own
Day :10 In Jaisalmer
  • Morning after breakfast at the hotel, proceed for sightseeing of Jaisalmer city visit Jaisalmer Fort, Nathmalji-Ki-Haveli and Patwaon ki Haveli, three beautifully sculptured Jain temples built between the 12th and 13th Centuries; and five interconnected palaces. The fort is approached through four gateways - Akhaiya Pol, Ganesh Pol, Suraj Pol and Hawa Pol. Overnight stay at hotel in Jaisalmer
Day :11 Jaisalmer – Bikaner
  • This morning after breakfast you will depart for Bikaner by surface approx 300 kms / 6 hrs. On arrival in Bikaner you will check in at your hotel. Evening will be at your leisure or free for shopping for local handicrafts in the market of Bikaner. Overnight stay at hotel
Day :12 Bikaner – Mandawa
  • Morning depart for half day sightseeing of Bikaner visiting Junagarh fort, Camel Breeding Farm & Deshnok Temple. Late afternoon you will proceed for Mandawa by surface appox 210 kms / 4 hrs. Check in at your hotel upon arrival in Mandawa. Overnight stay at hotel
Day :13 Mandawa – Delhi
  • Morning depart for half day excursion of Mandwa and visit Havelis famous for wall painting. Late afternoon you will proceed for Delhi by surface appox 250 kms / 5 hrs. Check in at your hotel upon arrival in Delhi. Overnight stay at hotel
